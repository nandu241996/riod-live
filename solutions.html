Agri-Live

Get your agribusiness optimised and integrated with our array of smart sensors and nodes to enable wireless monitoring in order to be up-to-date and take informed decisions in field produce, livestock maintenance, machinery & equipment status and much more.

Machine-Live

Knowing and understanding the performance of your machineries is key to maintain quality of production, additionally it is needed to be known in real-time. With the help of sense-I and our dedicated cloud platform you can view as well as detect any deviations and take necessary steps to mitigate the problems with required solutions.

Industry-Live

It is key to have a hollistic view of the daily updates, alerts and operations within an indsutry. Infra-Live

With Infra Live monitoring systems you can obtain real-time data collected and compiled from the various systems and equipments within your infrastructure. You can set regular notifications and alerts based on set level of the key parameters. This enables you to reduce resources and cost and increase efficiency and productivity.

Utilities provide the basic necessasities in the morden world and need to have a 24x7 operation. Activity-Live

Tracking and monitoring and getting the data at your fingertips is a necessary asset in the current generation. Getting the right data and taking the important decisions helps you stay successful in your business.
