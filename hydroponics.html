Hydroponics

Hydroponics or soil-less cultivation is the future of agriculture. This method of farming is ideal for urban spaces. It is crucial to monitor and check the water flow and also maintain the nutrient levels in the crops planted.

With our hydroponic specific sensors we can check the data across each crop pod and using these set levels we can also control the desired temperature, humidity, lighting and also the enable automation of nutrient dosage. Our dedicated platform will help you monitor and view these values from anywhere.
